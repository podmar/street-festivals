Berlin street festivals

Berlin is a vibrant metropolis in the center of Europe. Known for its avangarde art scene, night and street life, it continues to attract visitors from around - and beyond - the continent.

The city still remembers the turbulence of its recent history. This is what brings up a meaning and background to its expressive nature and love for individualism, freedom, joy of life.
